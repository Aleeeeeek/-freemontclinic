Understanding Therapy

Psychotherapy?

Psychotherapy is a method of addressing mental and emotional difficulties through structured conversations with a trained professional, such as a psychologist, psychiatrist, or licensed therapist.

People often call it talk therapy, counseling, or psychosocial support.

In the process of therapy, you gain a clearer understanding of the challenges you face and the ways your thoughts, feelings, and actions influence your overall well-being.

Through guided discussion, you develop healthier ways to navigate life's stressors and learn practical strategies that help you regain a sense of stability and control.
